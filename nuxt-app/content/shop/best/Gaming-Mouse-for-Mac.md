### What is the difference between an optical and laser sensor in a gaming mouse?

 Optical sensors use LED light and a photodiode to track movement, while laser sensors use a laser diode. Laser sensors generally provide higher DPI and better precision on various surfaces but can cause more cursor jitter. 


### Why is DPI important in a gaming mouse?

 DPI (dots per inch) determines how sensitive the mouse is, affecting the cursor's movement speed and accuracy. Higher DPI allows for quicker movements, but some gamers might find it uncomfortable.
