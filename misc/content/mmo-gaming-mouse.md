### What is MMO Gaming Mouse?

An MMO (Massively Multiplayer Online) gaming mouse is specifically designed for players engaging in massively multiplayer online games. It typically features extra buttons for skills, spells, or other in-game actions, allowing quick and easy access. 


### How does an MMO gaming mouse differ from a regular mouse?

An MMO gaming mouse differs from a regular mouse primarily in its design and functionality. Traditional mice usually have 2-3 buttons (left, right, and sometimes a scroll wheel), while MMO gaming mice boast up to 12 or more programmable buttons, typically aligned along the side or top of the mouse. This allows for more efficient and precise control within MMO games.

### What is the ideal DPI for an MMO gaming mouse?

DPI (dots per inch) is a measure of the sensitivity of the mouse. For MMO gaming, you may want a mouse with adjustable DPI, allowing you to set it according to your preferences. Typically, a higher DPI means faster cursor movements, while a lower DPI offers more precision.

### How do I clean an MMO gaming mouse?

To clean an MMO gaming mouse, first disconnect it from your computer. Use a soft, damp cloth to gently wipe down the surface. Avoid getting liquid inside any ports or crevices. For a deeper clean, you can use a can of compressed air to blow away dust and debris from the buttons and scroll wheel. If necessary, you may use a cotton swab dipped in rubbing alcohol to clean around the buttons, but be cautious not to apply too much pressure or get liquid inside the mouse.

No, you can use an MMO gaming mouse with almost any type of keyboard. However, some gamers prefer to use a full-sized mechanical keyboard with programmable macro keys for even more efficient gameplay in MMOs.
